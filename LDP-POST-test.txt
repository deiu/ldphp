Try to create an LDP container at https://test.data.fm/dir1/ by doing a POST with:

Content-Type: text/turtle

and this raw payload:

@prefix dcterms: <http://purl.org/dc/terms/>.
@prefix ldp: <http://www.w3.org/ns/ldp#>.
<>
   a ldp:Container ;
   dcterms:title "A new container" .



You can also try to create an LDP resource after at https://test.data.fm/dir1/res1 by doing a POST with:

Content-Type: text/turtle

and this raw payload:

@prefix dcterms: <http://purl.org/dc/terms/>.
@prefix ldp: <http://www.w3.org/ns/ldp#>.
<>
   a ldp:Resource ;
   dcterms:title "A new resource" .
